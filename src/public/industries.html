Industry Expertise

We work where supply chain mistakes carry real consequences.

Troer supports complex operations that need practical control over procurement, contractor delivery, and operational risk. We are strongest in sectors where timelines, compliance, and resilience cannot be left to chance.

What clients usually need from us

  • Central visibility across fragmented suppliers and contractors
  • Faster escalation when delivery or compliance risks appear
  • Hands-on support rather than high-level advisory reports
  • Clearer systems and stronger control for scaling teams

Sector-by-sector operational support.

Every sector creates its own version of supply chain pressure. We tailor the intervention to the operating reality, the pace of risk, and the level of control your team actually needs.

01 / NHS & Healthcare

Protect the flow of critical stock and support services.

Healthcare teams cannot afford fragmented procurement, missing visibility, or slow-moving contractor issues. We help build tighter control around supply continuity, supplier coordination, and high-stakes operational response.

  • Reduce procurement fragmentation across departments and sites
  • Support contractor governance in regulated environments
  • Improve visibility around shortages, delays, and escalation
02 / Construction & Infrastructure

Keep projects moving when coordination is under strain.

Infrastructure projects stall when contractor compliance slips, deliveries miss sequence, or commercial control gets blurred. We help teams stabilise delivery across moving parts that often fail to stay aligned.

  • Bring contractor oversight and milestone control into one view
  • Reduce delivery friction across suppliers and site teams
  • Support high-value project environments under timeline pressure
03 / Enterprise Operations

Replace disconnected reporting with operational clarity.

Enterprise teams often know the pain points but lack a practical way to connect systems, teams, and decisions. Troer helps create the visibility and operational discipline needed to make control stick.

  • Improve decision-making with cleaner operational reporting
  • Reduce dependence on manual spreadsheets and patchwork workflows
  • Support procurement, logistics, and stakeholder alignment
04 / Manufacturing & Scaling SMEs

Build a supply chain structure that can grow without breaking.

Growing organisations often outpace their systems before they realise it. We help put the fundamentals in place early so scaling does not create hidden waste, confusion, or unreliable delivery.

  • Move from reactive purchasing to stronger procurement control
  • Create cleaner supplier processes and clearer ownership
  • Support teams moving toward more mature operational systems
